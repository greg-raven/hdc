Join us for a fun, safe, and family-friendly cycling event with ride options for all experience levels. Riders can register online at Active.com.

Ride Options:

10 Mile --- Family Fun Ride

32 Mile --- The Tour

62 Mile --- The Grand Tour

Whether you're riding with family, friends, or pushing yourself with a longer distance, there's a route for everyone. Don't miss the chance to be part of this great cycling tradition in the Victor Valley.
