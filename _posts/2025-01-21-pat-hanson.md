Apple Valley resident and advocate Patricia "Pat" Hanson has died and "reunited with her husband in eternity" her loved ones said.

Hanson's daughter-in-law, Misti Thornton, told the *Daily Press* that one of Pat's friends remarked that Pat "Blessed many of our lives with her quick wit and her never-ending humor, smiles, and laugh."

Pat, 90, died on Jan. 21 and was reunited with her husband, Chuck, who died on July 17, 2016 at age 85, her family said.
