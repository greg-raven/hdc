To help us make future local cycling events even better, the folks at the Victor Valley Bicycle Tour (VVBT) would love to hear your feedback. If you participated, please take a few minutes to complete their short survey. Your insights will help them improve the experience for all participants.

We hope you enjoyed the ride and the community spirit. The VVBT is an all-volunteer community lead event. 

Click here to take the [2025 VVBT Rider Survey](https://forms.office.com/pages/responsepage.aspx?id=DQSIkWdsW0yxEjajBLZtrQAAAAAAAAAAAAO__YDRdu1UNFY1UVdISFNFRlBaTFVKVUVSNE02RUlCMi4u&route=shorturl).

Your opinion matters, and they truly appreciate your time and support.
